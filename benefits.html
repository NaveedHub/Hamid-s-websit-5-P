Whatever your views, there's no doubt that travelling by
car has both many advantages, and a lot of disadvantages.
To begin withthe speed, we can move quickly from one
place to another. We don't have to waste our time
and wait for a bus. If we want to go to the hospital
we do not need to go to the bus station and wait.
